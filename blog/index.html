What is AI Lead Capture and Why Vancouver Local Businesses Are Using It

By Sujith Sunil · Nexteps · June 2026 · 5 min read

If you've visited a business website recently and had a chat window pop up that felt surprisingly helpful — not a menu of buttons, but an actual conversation — there's a good chance you were talking to an AI assistant.

For a long time, tools like this were only accessible to large companies with big tech budgets. That's changed. Local businesses in Vancouver and Surrey are now using AI lead capture to compete with larger competitors, generate more revenue from their existing website traffic, and stop losing enquiries after hours.

Here's a plain-English explanation of what it is and how it works.

What AI lead capture actually is

AI lead capture is a system that sits on your business website, has real conversations with visitors, answers their questions, and collects their contact information — automatically, at any hour, without any staff involvement.

It's different from an old-school chatbot in one important way: it doesn't work from a fixed script. It understands what the visitor is actually asking and responds appropriately, drawing on real knowledge of your business. It reads like a conversation with a knowledgeable person, not a help menu.

When a visitor is ready to get in touch — wants a quote, wants to book, wants someone to call them — the AI captures their name and phone number and sends it to the business owner immediately as an email. The owner wakes up to leads instead of missed opportunities.

How it's different from a regular contact form

A contact form is passive. It sits on a page and waits for someone to be motivated enough to stop, type out their situation, and submit it. Most people aren't at that point when they first visit your website. They're exploring. They have questions. They're not ready to commit to filling out a form.

An AI assistant meets the visitor where they are. It engages them in conversation, answers their specific question, and naturally collects their information as part of the interaction — so by the time the visitor leaves their name and number, they already feel like they've had a useful interaction with your business.

Contact forms typically convert between 1 and 3 percent of website visitors. Conversational AI lead capture converts 3 to 8 percent — because it removes friction and meets visitors at the moment they have a question, not after they've already decided to commit.

What it's actually trained on

The key difference between a useful AI assistant and a generic chatbot is what it knows. A generic chatbot knows nothing about your business. It asks the visitor to choose from a menu of options and routes them to a page they could have found themselves.

A custom AI assistant — the kind Nexteps builds for local businesses in Metro Vancouver — is trained on your specific business. It knows:

  • Your services and how you describe them
  • Your hours and location
  • Your service area (which neighbourhoods or cities you cover)
  • Your team and their specialisations
  • Your pricing approach and what affects cost
  • The most common questions your customers ask
  • What to say in an emergency situation
  • What it should and shouldn't answer (for regulated industries like mortgage or law)

It doesn't guess or make things up. It answers from what it knows about your business, and redirects to you for anything outside its knowledge.

Who is using it in Vancouver right now

The local businesses seeing the biggest benefit from AI lead capture in Metro Vancouver tend to share a few characteristics: they get website visitors but not enough enquiries, they miss calls while busy with clients, and they don't have the budget or need for a full-time receptionist.

That describes a lot of businesses across a wide range of industries:

Dental clinics in Vancouver and Surrey

High call volume, insurance questions that take time to answer, and patients who search at night when offices are closed. AI handles the routine questions and captures new patient leads 24/7.

HVAC and trades companies across Metro Vancouver

Technicians are on the road and can't answer every call. AI captures service requests, emergency calls, and heat pump enquiries while the team is working.

Mortgage brokers in BC

Leads in the mortgage space go to whoever responds first. AI ensures no website visitor leaves without being engaged — and captures their information before they move on to a competitor.

Law firms

Legal situations often become urgent outside business hours. AI handles intake questions and captures potential client information so the firm can follow up in the morning.

What the business owner actually does

This is the part that surprises most business owners when they first hear about it. Your involvement is minimal.

You get an email when a lead is captured. The email has the person's name, phone number, and what they were asking about. You call them back. That's it.

No portal to log into. No dashboard to monitor. No new system to learn. The AI does the work. You make the calls.
