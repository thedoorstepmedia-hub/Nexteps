Why Nexteps exists

A good-looking website that loses leads isn't an asset. It's decoration.

I'm Sujith, a web designer based in Vancouver. I started building websites for friends and family and kept noticing the same thing. The site would look great, cost real money, and nothing would change. No more calls. No more leads. Just a nicer homepage.

A website that doesn't connect you to your customers the moment they arrive isn't doing its job. So I built the tools to fix that. Nexteps is what came out of it. A system that sits on your existing site and makes sure every visitor who has a question gets an answer, and every lead lands in your inbox.

We take businesses that have already taken their first step and take them to the next level. One step above where they are. One step ahead of who they're competing with.
